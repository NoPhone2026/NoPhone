Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 6, 2026

Who we are

NoPhone ("we", "us", "our") operates the website www.thenophone.com. This policy explains how we handle your personal data.

What we collect

We collect only your email address when you voluntarily sign up for our mailing list. We do not collect any other personal data, use cookies, or track your browsing activity.

Why we collect it

We use your email address solely to notify you about The NoPhone product launch and related updates. We will not use it for any other purpose.

How we process it

Your email address is stored and processed by Mailchimp (The Rocket Science Group LLC), our email service provider. Mailchimp processes data in accordance with their own privacy policy. By signing up, your data may be transferred to and stored in the United States.

Your rights

Under the GDPR, you have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time. Every email we send includes an unsubscribe link. You can also contact us directly to request deletion of your data.

Data retention

We retain your email address until you unsubscribe or request deletion, or until we no longer need it for the purpose stated above.

Contact

For any privacy-related questions, contact us at contact@thenophone.com.
